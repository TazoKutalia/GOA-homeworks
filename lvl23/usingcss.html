Introduction

Global warming refers to the long-term heating of Earth's climate system due to human activities, primarily fossil fuel burning, which increases heat-trapping greenhouse gas levels in Earth's atmosphere.

Causes

  • Burning Fossil Fuels
  • Deforestation
  • Industrial Activities
  • Agriculture

Effects

Global warming has numerous effects including rising sea levels, more extreme weather events, and loss of biodiversity. It impacts ecosystems and human societies globally.

Solutions

  • Reducing Emissions
  • Using Renewable Energy
  • Conserving Energy
  • Protecting Forests
